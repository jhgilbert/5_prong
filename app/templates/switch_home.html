Welcome to Switch.

Switch encourages you to use your time well — without any intimidating to-do lists.

Learn more

Just flip the switches on and off as they apply.

Going on a date? That's LOVE. Going on a date to volunteer at a food bank? That's LOVE and HELP. Going on a date where you volunteer for Habitat for Humanity and spend your day honing your construction skills and toning your muscles? That's ... all five. Dang, you're good.

Remember, you're a person too, and you can HELP yourself by doing the dishes, going to the DMV, or otherwise taking care of yourself. It's not just about other people. Housework is a great way to HELP and MOVE at the same time!

When you're finished with any given category, switch it off. That's it!
